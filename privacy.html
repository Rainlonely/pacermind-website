Privacy Policy

Your run, your data, your mind.

This policy explains what data Pacermind collects, how AI features use it, who receives it, and how you control consent.

1. Data Controller

Pacermind is developed and operated by Rainlonely Studio (“we”, “us”, “our”). For any privacy questions, contact rainlonely@me.com.

2. Data We Collect and Access

  • Workout and health/fitness data you authorize from Apple Health, such as distance, duration, pace, cadence, heart rate, splits, and related workout metadata.
  • Workout metadata and activity details you choose to sync from Strava, if connected.
  • Profile and training inputs you enter in the app, such as goals, race information, weekly availability, notes, and training preferences.
  • Technical and app settings data necessary to provide the service, such as app language, consent version, and device/app environment details relevant to rendering and requests.

We may also infer training summaries from the data above, such as weekly load, execution quality, and AI-ready workout context. Pacermind does not require your real name to use the app.

3. How We Collect Data

  • Directly from you when you enter profile details, goals, notes, and plan preferences.
  • From Apple Health and Strava only after you choose to connect or authorize those sources.
  • Automatically from app usage and device context when needed to render features, persist settings, and process requests securely.

4. How We Use Data

  • To import, display, and organize your workouts, plans, journals, and history.
  • To generate AI-assisted training plans, workout insights, weekly journal insights, and coach continuation flows.
  • To maintain app settings, consent records, subscriptions, debugging, and service reliability.

5. AI Features, Sharing & Consent

Certain AI features send selected workout-related data, health/fitness metrics, derived training summaries, and your text inputs to Rainlonely Studio’s servers and to OpenAI in order to generate plans and insights. We request your explicit permission in-app before any such AI transfer occurs. You can decline, or revoke consent later in Settings.

  • Recipients: Rainlonely Studio / PacerMind server infrastructure (relay/processor) and OpenAI.
  • Purpose: AI plan generation, workout analysis, weekly insight generation, and coach continuation features.
  • Protection: We require service providers that receive this data to provide the same or equal protection of user data as described in this policy and applicable law.

6. Data Storage, Retention & Deletion

Workout records, plan data, journal data, and consent state may be stored on your device and, where necessary to deliver network-backed features, in our service infrastructure for a limited period. Removing app data, disconnecting sources, revoking permissions, or uninstalling the app will stop future collection from those sources. Some server logs and request records may be retained for security, debugging, fraud prevention, and legal compliance.

7. User Controls

  • Revoke HealthKit permissions at any time in iOS Settings → Health → Data Access.
  • Disconnect Strava inside Pacermind Settings.
  • Decline or revoke AI Data Sharing in Pacermind Settings before future AI requests are sent.

8. Children’s Privacy

Pacermind is intended for users aged 16 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from children below the age permitted by applicable law.

9. Policy Updates

When we update this policy, we may revise the in-app consent flow, update the consent version, and note the revision date below.
